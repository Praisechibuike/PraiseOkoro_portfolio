Who I Am

5 Months of Building

Hi there! I am Praise Okoro, a passionate and driven web development student currently enrolled in a web development bootcamp. I have always been fascinated by the intersection of creativity and technology, and web development perfectly bridges that gap for me.

Before discovering my passion for coding, I built my foundation in project management, where I developed strong skills in organization, problem-solving, and delivering results. Now, I’m channeling that structured mindset into building websites that are not only functional but thoughtfully designed and user-friendly. In my free time, I enjoy making music, exploring creativity through sound, and experimenting with new ideas.

I am learning web development because I want to build products that make a real difference in people's lives. My goal is to become a full-stack developer who can take an idea from concept to a fully deployed application. Every line of code I write brings me one step closer to that goal.
